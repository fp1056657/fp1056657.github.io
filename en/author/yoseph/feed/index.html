Gaar

GAAR is an NGO that has been fighting for animal welfare for more than 15 years. He works in Campinas, promoting awareness for responsible dog and cat guarding, a continuous work of castration, vaccination and donation of these animals, hoping that in the near future abandoned animals will be a thing of the past.

We do not have a shelter and so we do not collect animals. But we can guide and intermediate veterinary procedures at affordable prices for all those who are motivated to lend a helping hand to an abandoned animal.

Centro Infantil Boldrini

The Boldrini Children’s Center is a philanthropic hospital specializing in pediatric oncology and hematology that currently has a cancer cure rate of 80%. Of private and non-profit nature, the institution is of municipal, state and federal public utility, therefore, belongs to the community.
The focus on teaching and research aims at improving and personalizing the treatment, seeking more effective results and increasing the chances of cure. The name of the hospital honors the pediatrician Domingos Adhemar Boldrini, who died in 1976, who provided relevant services in the city of Campinas.
